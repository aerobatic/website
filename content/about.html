Our Values

This is what we're all about. Whether we're small or enormous, these are the things that will always be true of Aerobatic and how we work.

The customer is the hero.

Two things wrapped up in this. The first is that Aerobatic is all about empowering our customers. To do that, we go the extra mile to understand them. It's never about us - it's always about our customers. The second is that no matter what we’re doing, if a customer needs our help, we jump. We go the extra mile to help.

Speak softly, carry a big stick.

No egos. No heroics. We’re humble, yet confident, pros that know how to get things done. We trust each other to operate the same way.

Penny wise. Pound wise.

We’re frugal whether we’ve got one dollar or billions of them. Frugality necessitates creative solutions and a scrappy, underdog mentality. That will always be us.

Life happens.

We’re dads, moms, brothers, sisters, friends. Work is important – a vital outlet for our passion, creativity, and competitiveness. We welcome everyone - hobbyist and professional coders of all stripes that see the web as the greatest distribution channel ever. We’ll always be a place that understands and encourages the fact that life happens, outside and inside of work.
